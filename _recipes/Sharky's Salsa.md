## Ingredients

* 8 1/2 pounds or 12 large tomatoes, quartered
* 3 onions
* 3 green bell peppers
* 6 - 23 hot peppers
* 1 1/2 teaspoons cumin
* 1 1/2 teaspoons oregano
* 2 Tablespoons salt
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup vinegar

cook for another 35 minutes. Makes 3 quarts.
