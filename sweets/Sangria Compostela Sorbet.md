## Ingredients

5 pears, peeled, cored, and chopped
1 tart green apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
2 stalks lemongrass, pounded
1 small sprig rosemary
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup Albariño
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup light corn syrup
2 Tablespoons lemon juice
2 Tablespoons sake

## Directions

In a medium saucepan, combine the pears, apple, lemongrass, rosemary, sugar, Albariño, water, and corn syrup. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce the heat to medium-low. Simmer until the pears are tender, 10 - 15 minutes. Remove the lemongrass and rosemary, and puree until smooth. Strain through a sieve into a medium bowl, discarding any remaining solids. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Stir in the lemon juice and sake immediately before churning in an ice cream machine according to manufacturer instructions.
